CHAPTER 13: THE CIRCUITRY

Every year, an electrifying event of Anvesha has captured the attention of tech enthusiasts and curious minds alike â€“ the Black Box Circuit Competition.

 
This unique and mind-bending contest challenges the participants to work out the hidden circuit within an opaque black box. The catch? The only tools at their disposal are varying voltage and current.


The Black Box Circuit Competition combines puzzle-solving, electrical and circuital elements, and a dash of deduction. Imagine a room filled with eager participants, each armed with a single goal: to deduce the intricate circuit concealed within the black box.


The setup is simple yet enigmatic. Contestants receive a mysterious black box devoid of any visible clues. Inside the box lies a unique electric circuit configuration, waiting to be unraveled. Armed with a power supply, participants must experiment with varying the input in the form of voltage and current to guess the secrets within. The challenge is to decipher the circuit's components and connections solely by observing these cryptic responses as the output. It's a thrilling blend of science, deduction, and some trial and error.
