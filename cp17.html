CHAPTER 17: THE NOTICE


Being a traveller in quest of knowledge isn’t always easy. For all the wisdom it holds, the forest is treacherous, and your journey is fraught with challenges. You’ve just about resigned yourself to another lonely night underneath the canopy when you hear the murmur of voices up ahead, and glimpse a warm, amber glow diffusing through the trees. You move towards it, the light a beacon in the gathering dusk.


After trudging alone through the forest for days, even the raucousness of the tavern is music to your ears. A notice on the wall catches your eye. You move closer for a better look:

JIGYASA


Ready for a battle of wits against the sharpest minds? Join us for Jigyasa, the open general quiz of Anvesha, with the quizmaster Major Chandrakant Nair. Go solo or interfere constructively with up to two other people (teams of 3) as you tackle brain-teasing questions spanning the spectrum of science and beyond. Exciting prizes (prize pool of 12k) await the winners!


The quiz will be on October 22nd. Make sure to register before October 8th. Further details will be shared with the participants.
